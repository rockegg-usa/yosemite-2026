🌊 Mist Trail → Vernal Fall
Happy Isles Parking3 mi round trip1,000 ft gain600 granite steps
ParkingHappy Isles Trailhead (free, bear boxes on site) · 📍 37.7349, -119.5665 — arrive before 9 AM to get a spot
BackupIf full → Curry Village · 📍 37.7361, -119.5686 → free shuttle stop #16
Bear BoxAll food and scented items MUST go in the bear boxes at the trailhead — bears will break car windows
TrailTrailhead → footbridge viewpoint (1.2 km — easy turnaround option) → Mist/JMT fork (1.4 km) → 600 granite steps → top of Vernal Fall (2.4 km) → descend via John Muir Trail (recommended — easier on knees)
GearRain jacket essential — the mist drenches everything. Grippy shoes. Go up on Mist Trail, come down on John Muir Trail.
⚠️ Safety: Never enter the river or Emerald Pool — currents are deadly despite calm appearance. Steps are very slippery; descending is more dangerous than ascending. Stay focused.
